## Posting Schedule

### Launch Week
- **Day 1**: Launch announcement (all platforms)
- **Day 2**: Feature highlight
- **Day 3**: Technical deep dive
- **Day 4**: User testimonial/case study
- **Day 5**: Community thank you

### Ongoing (Weekly)
- **Monday**: Feature update or tip
- **Wednesday**: Technical content
- **Friday**: Community highlight or user story

## Engagement Tips

1. **Respond Quickly**: Answer questions within 2-4 hours
2. **Be Authentic**: Share your journey and challenges
3. **Provide Value**: Share tips, insights, or learnings
4. **Engage**: Like, comment, and share relevant content
5. **Build Community**: Connect with users and contributors
